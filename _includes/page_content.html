Technical debt

Technology is moving fast, and it's easy to end up with an application that's outdated. This can lead to degraded performances, increased security risks, and decreased productivity for your team.

I've made a habit of handling application updates and data migration so that your team can focus on the features that your end customers interact with.

Tooling

While easily overlooked, efficient tooling improves your team's ability to deliver work of quality and how often they do it. On top of that, automation can help keep your application up to date and safe.

From custom-made command-line developer tools to setting up continuous integration/ delivery, there are many ways that I can help improve your workflow.
